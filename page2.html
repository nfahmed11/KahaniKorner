Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

What age range are the books for?

Our books are designed for children ages 0-8, but language learners of all ages enjoy them! The board books are perfect for toddlers, while the storybooks grow with your child.

Do I need to know Urdu to read them?

Not at all! Every book includes English, Urdu script, and Roman Urdu (Urdu written in English letters) so anyone can read along, regardless of fluency.

Where do you ship?

We currently ship worldwide! Free shipping is available for all orders within the US. International rates are calculated at checkout.

What comes in the subscription box?

Each monthly box is a surprise theme packed with a new bilingual book, vocabulary flashcards, vinyl stickers, and an activity guide to keep learning fun.

Can I buy in bulk for my classroom?

Yes! We love supporting educators. Please contact us for special bulk pricing and lesson plan resources.
