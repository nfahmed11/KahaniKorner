Frequently Asked Questions

What is Kahani Korner?

We create Urdu–English storybooks with activities, songs, and games so learning continues after the last page.

What age group are the books for?

Toddlers to early readers — with activities that scale up as kids grow.

Are your books available in both Urdu and English?

Yes! We use Urdu, English, and Roman Urdu to support mixed-fluency families.

What comes in a bundle?

Storybook + stickers or activities (varies), and access to digital extras.

Our Story

Kahani Korner was born at 3 a.m.—somewhere between bouncing a baby and wondering why bilingual books felt like homework. So we made our own: books that speak your family’s languages and don’t stop teaching when you stop reading. With sing-alongs, flashcards, and games, we make bilingual learning effortless and joyful.
